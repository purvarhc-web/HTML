Explore New York City

New York City is a major U.S. metropolis known for its diverse culture and global influence in finance, media, and the arts. It is a city of over 8.8 million people, divided into five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island. The city's economy is a global hub for finance and media, and its landmarks include the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and the Empire State Building.
